It's Only the Result That Counts   
人啊～ 都是成敗論英雄的

November 3, 2005 by Paul Tester   
2005年11月3日 由 Paul Tester創作

      As the only girl in the asset management group of an international financial company in London, a savagely competitive environment where being female was rarely an advantage, Lorraine had to continually tread the thin line between being 'one of the team' and becoming too familiar with the other men, some of whom were not above trying to take advantage of her. In her late twenties, just above average height, with what she considered a 'good figure' she took great care to always maintain a business-like appearance with smart clothes and formal hair styles, and matched this with a formal and business-like attitude to her colleagues. She had acute hearing and could lip-read tolerably well, so she knew much of what was said about her. The men might speculate, but they weren't going to find out what she wore under her skirts.

這裡是位於倫敦的一間跨國財務公司的資產管理事業群。想要在這種既野蠻又高度競爭的環境中生存，身為女性恐怕從來都不是什麼優勢。以若蓮的工作環境來說，她得要小心翼翼的拿捏分寸，時時小心男性下屬可能的佔便宜。  二十多歲，中等身高的她，熟知要善用商務的打扮、聰明俐落的衣著和正式的髮型，在同事面前打造出自己專業的形象。 她不但聽力好，也擅讀身邊人的唇語，也因此她多少知道這些男同事在八卦她哪些五四三。比方說，就算不可能真的知道，這些男的依然三不五時會猜猜若蓮今天穿什麼內褲之類的無聊話題。

      One lunchtime they were celebrating winning a major new contract, due at least in part to the client liking to see Lorraine in tight skirts, something she was happy to indulge, though he wasn't going to get any further. Lorraine celebrated as well as any of the team, drinking her 3 pints, her limit for lunchtimes, with the best of them. She was one of the first to finish and head back to the office, as she had a teleconference with New York set up for after lunch. Two of the men left with her, and as they got the in the lift Lorraine checked her watch. Ten minutes before her conference, not much time to spare, and she hated being rushed. Then the lift stopped suddenly and the emergency lighting came on, but nothing else happened. Lorraine glared at the lift controls, willing it to get moving again.

某天，事業群的人一同午餐，慶祝剛簽好的新約（不過有些男同事是想藉機欣賞若蓮的窄裙啦）。若蓮跟其他同事一樣開酒慶祝，喝了三杯。不過，若蓮因為待會有個視訊會議要開，所以提前跟兩個男同事一起先返回辦公室。在電梯裡，若蓮瞄了一下手錶。哇！剩十分鐘而已，唉！ 她討厭匆匆忙忙的感覺。 可是，電梯突然停了下來，緊急照明燈也亮了。 然後，一切都停了下來。 若蓮張大眼瞪著電梯按鈕，希望電梯可以趕快又動起來。

      In the lift with Lorraine were colleagues Roger and Adam, a middle-aged man from personnel, and two executive PA's, ultra-efficient women about fifty. All looked mildly concerned, and one PA began trying to call someone on the emergency phone. Eventually she got through, and then announced, "They're looking into it. It will be five or ten minutes before they know what's wrong."

跟若蓮一起回來的，是同事羅傑和亞當。除此之外，還有一個人事部的中年肥宅，兩個看起來很俐落的女執行秘書。 大家都有點擔心電梯卡住，其中一個秘書試著用電梯裡面的緊急電話撥打求救。 好不容易接通詢問了狀況之後，她跟大家說：「嗯…..外面已經在處理了，不過可能得花個五到十分鐘他們才能知道到底電梯哪裡壞掉喔！」

      "Who's got meetings due?" she continued. "I'll get someone to contact your departments and get them re-scheduled."

        秘書繼續問說：「誰等一下有會？」「我會轉達你的狀況，讓你的部門幫你重約開會時間。」

      Reluctantly Lorraine suggested that Justin and her secretary could try to deal with New York until she was free. She was fuming at the delay, because she did not want to lose control of this project, and knew that Justin would jump at the chance to get involved. Another, minor, annoyance was that she wanted a pee. Nothing desperate, but after all that beer she would absolutely have to go before starting the conference, which would be more time wasted. Peeing was another thing she was defensive about; even if she were bursting she would never leave a meeting or presentation for a pee, though the men often did.


若蓮不太情願，不過也只好請秘書轉達給她的秘書和另一位同事賈斯汀，跟紐約的連線會議在她脫身之前還是照常舉行。若蓮不想失去對這個專案的掌控，所以她很討厭遲到。現在可好了，賈斯汀大概會趁虛而入吧？ 不過，現在還有另一件惱人的事情，她想尿尿了。 尿意還沒很強，不過三杯啤酒下肚，在進會議室前她是真的需要去解決一下，可是這樣又要浪費掉一些時間了。 上廁所對她來說是一件頗為抗拒的事情，不像男生總是會議或簡報中途跑廁所，有控制欲的若蓮不管有多急都不會在會議中途離席的。

      Ten minutes later everyone had passed on instructions to their assistants. There was still no news about the lift except that 'they were working on it,' and Lorraine had nothing more to do except to resign herself to the situation. She tried to ignore that she wanted to pee quite badly, a lot worse than when the lift had stopped. Unconsciously she had started shifting her weight from foot to foot, both in general frustration and to try to ease her need to pee. Realizing what she was doing, she forced herself to stop, leaning against the wall of the lift with her legs crossed, trying hard to look relaxed and unconcerned.

十分鐘過去，電梯裡面的人也一一把該交代給助理的事情們透過緊急電話傳了出去，不過，除了正在維修中之外，電梯到底怎麼樣了，他們還是一無所知。若蓮只好不去想這個糟糕的狀況。跟剛剛走進電梯時相比，她很想要忽略這個越來越急的感覺，下意識的開始輪流挪動雙腳，想減輕自己的尿意。若蓮突然發現自己這個不雅的動作，所以立刻靜了下來，靠在電梯邊牆，交疊雙腿，想試著讓自己看起來什麼事都沒有。

      She analyzed her situation; three pints of beer in an hour, ending about fifteen minutes ago, when she was already swimming with coffee after the morning presentation. Her kidneys would be working at maximum rate now, pumping pee into her bladder which was already rather full, to say the least. There was nothing she could do except hold on for the few more minutes it would take the to fix the lift, and not think about wanting a loo, which would only make it worse. Keep her legs crossed to take as much strain as possible off her bladder, which, she could not delude herself any longer, was already bursting. She wished she had something to do to make the time pass more quickly, and take her mind off wanting a loo.

她分析了一下自己的狀況：十五分鐘前，在上個小時的餐敘中喝了三杯啤酒，還有早上簡報之後的咖啡，這下子她的腎臟應該是全速工作了，認真的把尿液源源不斷打進那個快滿的膀胱裡頭。不過，除了忍住，不要去想廁所，然後等電梯修好，她也沒別的辦法了。夾緊雙腿的同時，她好像覺得再也騙不了自己膀胱好漲好漲的事實。 有什麼方法可以讓時間過得快一點呢？ 有什麼方法可以讓她不要想著上廁所呢？

      Don't think about loos, she told herself firmly. It had to be imagination, but she seemed to want to go more badly already. How long were they going to take to fix the lift? She wanted to phone and ask, but she knew really it would not do any good, and only draw attention to her anxiety. Management training had taught her that there are no problems, only opportunities, so this was a bad situation that she had to turn to her advantage. The only thing she could do was to not give the slightest sign she wanted a loo and show what control she had, so they would talk about her in awe: "Remember the lunchtime when Lorraine was trapped in the lift after drinking three pints, and she never needed to pee? What a girl! I don't know how she managed it."

「好了！別再想！」若蓮堅定的告訴自己。不過她好像又更想尿了。電梯到底什麼時候才會修好啊？ 若蓮想過去打緊急電話問個明白，可是她知道這樣子只是讓電梯裡面的其他人發覺她的緊張而已。 經理人的訓練是這麼說的：沒有問題，只有機會。 所以要轉危機為契機！ 換句話說，若蓮得把這個糟糕的處境變成她的優勢。 只是她能做的只有盡量隱藏自己想上廁所的跡象，這樣一來，其他同事之後對她的八卦就是：「ㄟㄟ，妳們記得若蓮喝了三杯啤酒結果被關在電梯的那天嗎？ 她都不用尿尿的耶！ 超強的啦！ 超佩服，我都不知道她是怎麼熬過來的說！」

      Except...she had only been stuck for half an hour and she was bursting, seriously bursting. Much longer and she was going to be desperate, really desperate, perhaps even worse, if there was a worse state than desperate. She didn't want to have to wait that long. She would rather be released now and just give them something to speculate about: "Wonder what Lorraine would have done if the lift had been stuck all afternoon? How long before she had to pee? Then what would she have done?"

不過，人總要回到現實。若蓮在電梯裡大概半小時，不過她已經快爆了，是真的要爆炸的那種。越等越久，也讓若蓮越來越絕望、越來越絕望。 她本來不用忍這麼久的，她超想尿尿，可是現在只能這樣用白日夢鼓勵自己：「挖！想想要是若蓮被困在電梯裡一整個下午呢？ 這樣她得憋多久啊？  她要怎麼辦？」

      Her speculation was stopped by one of the Exec PA's phoning to demand a progress report. The news wasn't good; they were still working on it, so another 15 or 20 minutes at least. The questions, 'How long before she had to pee? Then what would she have done?' might become horribly relevant. The best thing she could do was to take as much strain off her bladder as possible by sitting down. Pity the lift didn't have a carpet, but it looked clean, and sitting in the corner, legs straight in front of her, thighs and knees pressed together, was a lot easier on her bladder. The others copied her, resigned to the situation. Lorraine tried to think of the work she should be doing, but the need for a loo soon made it difficult to concentrate. It was getting serious; no, it was serious. She could not remember wanting to go as badly as this, and she would have given an awful lot to get to a loo right away.

若蓮的白日夢被其中一個執秘的對話打斷了，那個執秘要外面報告維修進度，不過沒什麼好消息。 電梯還在修，可能要再修個15到20分鐘。這下好了，還要等多久才有得尿？ 現在她要怎麼辦？ 若蓮想了想，現在她也只能坐下來，減輕一點對膀胱的壓力了。 這個電梯沒鋪地毯，不過還算乾淨。 如果坐在角落雙腳打直夾緊，應該幫助很大。 她先坐了下來，其他人也有樣學樣。若蓮想著工作來分心，但是想尿尿的念頭讓她越來越難專心，越來越難。若蓮想不到以前有哪次要憋到這種地步，就算有也是有個髒髒廁所可以讓她解決一下。

      If only there was some way she could make it easier to wait. She couldn't help thinking about holding between her legs; but to do that would be an admission of absolute desperation, of being about to wet herself. She had never done that when anyone might see, not even when she was a little girl. The only alternative was to sit on her heel, but could she manage that on the floor of a lift? She surreptitiously kicked off her high-heeled shoes and twisted round until she could fold her legs back under her. Having to keep her knees together, and her skirt pulled down, it wasn't as good as she had hoped. Her skirt was too tight, and she could not get her foot at the right angle for the heel to press really hard up between her legs. She tried shifting her position this way and that, but nothing was much better. All she was doing was drawing attention to herself, and worse, making her skirt tighter across her stomach, putting more, and very unwelcome, pressure on her bladder.

若蓮想著，有沒有其他可以幫忙憋久一點的方法呀？ 可是這樣子一直想不就承認了自己已經憋得很慘，而且可能快尿出來了嗎？ 從小女生開始，若蓮可就沒有在外人面前尿過褲子了。 有個方法可能有用：坐在腳踝上。可是這一招在椅子上可以，在電梯地板可以嗎？ 她偷偷地把高跟鞋踢掉，把雙腳側曲回來。把兩膝併攏靠邊、放下裙子，因為窄裙太緊了，效果好像沒那麼好，若蓮沒辦法把腳底調到按住腿間的位置。挪來挪去的結果不但不見效果，還讓窄裙不斷的勒緊自己的胃部，當然也一次又一次壓到自己的膀胱。

      When she did manage to get her heel pressing where she needed it, she was sitting bolt upright, a most unnatural position that she was horribly afraid made it very obvious what she was doing. She tried relaxing, leaning back against the side of the lift, but then her heel wasn't pressing where she wanted it to press. The few minutes she had been properly on her heel had felt so good that she just had to get back to that, however odd it looked. She consoled herself that none of the men in the lift would recognize her position for what it was, and if the women did, they would keep quiet and thankful that they were not in the same situation.

總算，若蓮找到一個可以堵住自己的姿勢了，不過他直挺挺地這也太不自然，大家會不會都注意到了？ 她想故做輕鬆，把自己靠在電梯邊上，可是這樣子她就沒有壓到該堵的地方了，可是那樣有用，感覺好好喔！ 所以他決定還是回到怪異的坐姿，然後跟自己說，電梯裡面的男生們不會懂為什麼她姿勢要這樣的啦！ 如果其他女孩子心有靈犀看了出來，她們應該會默默的謝天謝地，感謝自己現在沒有像若蓮一樣這麼窘迫。

      Ten minutes passed. Her need to pee had not got much worse, thanks to the pressure from her heel. She was starting to get cramp in her leg from sitting in such an awkward position, but she willed herself to bear this a bit longer, frightened of how badly she might want to pee if she was off her heel for any time. Her tight, knee length skirt, such an advantage in the morning's meeting, was now a real liability, making sitting on her heel so difficult. Her bladder area was aching really badly, much worse than five minutes ago, not helped by her skirt being so tight there. She risked pulling her skirt up a couple of inches, which let her move enough to both ease the cramp in her foot and bear down harder on her heel. She was going to have to wait a bit longer, bladder ache or not. That was a warning of just how full of pee she was getting, as if she didn't know that already.

又過了十分鐘，有了坐在腳踝的幫忙加壓，若蓮的尿意沒有繼續增長，可是她腳麻了。若蓮不敢改變姿勢，她怕一動，尿意又要澎湃而來。她那件在晨會中無往不利的及膝窄裙，現在變成了最大的負擔。因為裙子，她的膀胱痛得要命。她把裙擺往上拉了一點，讓她好挪動繃緊的雙腿。不管膀胱有多痛，她也只能等下去。膀胱的躁動，就像是怕若蓮不知道到底她有多想尿尿似的。

      Everyone in the lift had adopted a typically British attitude of staring at the floor in front of them, avoiding eye contact with their companions. This, Lorraine hoped, meant that none of them would realize how badly she wanted a loo, how much she was struggling to wait. It was also getting darker in the lift as the emergency lighting ran down, another welcome development. Lorraine would have been happy for it to be pitch dark, when she could have held herself without anyone seeing.

電梯裡的人都很有默契的拿出了英國派的修養，盯著眼前的地板，並且有禮貌的不要跟其他人眼神交會。也因此，若蓮覺得其他人應該都不會注意到她有多想上廁所，等得有多急了吧？ 同時，緊急照明燈也因為電力耗盡而暗了下來，嗯…..這是好事。若蓮很高興現在越來越黑了，這樣大家就不會看到她憋尿的模樣。

      "Can you ask how much longer they are likely to be?" Roger asked the PA who had taken charge of the telephone.

羅傑拜託那個負責講電話的執秘問說：「你可以幫忙問問還要等多久嗎？」

      She passed his question on, adding that it was getting darker and hotter in the lift. They all waited expectantly for the answer.

執秘轉達了這個問題，還跟外面說電梯裡頭現在是又暗又悶熱，大家都想要一個電梯修好的答案。

      "The first thing they tried hasn't worked," she relayed the news, "so it's going to be another fifteen minutes or so. They can reconnect the power now, so we will get some ventilation."

「維修人員嘗試的第一個方法失敗了」執行秘書轉達著：「所以可能又要再十五分鐘吧！不過他們有辦法把電源街上，所以我們呆會會有空調。」

      As she spoke the lights came on and the fan started, blowing cool air round the lift. None of this was good news for Lorraine. There went her dream of being able to hold her crutch in the dark. She wasn't kidding herself any more that adults didn't hold themselves; if she had been alone her hand would have been firmly between her legs, fingers holding back her pee as hard as she could. Now she would have to survive on her heel, however uncomfortable that was getting. She didn't need the air conditioning either. The hotter it was the more she would sweat, which would mean less pee, and the way the situation was looking, anything that meant even one drop less pee in her bladder would be a help.

執秘還在說著呢～ 電燈亮了、風扇也重新運轉，把外面的涼風帶進電梯。 只是這兩件對若蓮都不是好消息，這下她沒辦法在黑暗中憋尿了。 一個成年人忍不住這可不是開玩笑的說，要是只有她自己，她早就用雙手的最大力氣按住兩腿之間了。 不過，現在只能用腳踝。 若蓮想著她跟本不要風扇啊，熱一點才能多流一點汗。對若蓮來說，可以多出一滴汗，就代表膀胱可以少一滴尿，那可是天大的幫助好嗎！

      Another ten minutes, which seemed more like an hour. She wanted to pee really, really, badly, and her bladder was hurting more. This was getting beyond a joke, and Lorraine tried not to think what it would be like if they were not released soon. She would just have to go on waiting; there was nothing else she could do. It might hurt, but she was a big girl, and could bear a bit of pain. Wrapped up in her own misery, she had not noticed that Roger had been shuffling about until he took a deep breath and said- "I'm awfully sorry about this, but I have simply got to have a pee. I really can't wait another second."

十分鐘又十分鐘，對若蓮來說像一個小時一樣。膀胱越來越痛，也就越來越想尿。這不是開玩笑的，若蓮已經不敢想到底什麼時候她們才會被放出去了。她只能忍、忍、忍。 已經是個大女孩啦，那一點痛沒什麼的。 收拾了自己的牢騷，他才發現旁邊的羅傑早就抖抖抖，最後吸了一口大氣跟大家說：「我真的很抱歉，可是我真的要排泄一下，不能等了。」

      He faced the corner of the lift and unzipped his trousers, seeming to release a spurt of pee immediately, not giving anyone time to comment. "There's no drain hole, so I think some of you might have to stand up or get flooded," he continued. "I am really a sorry about this, but I could not have held out any longer."

        其他人還來不及說些什麼，羅傑已經對著電梯角落拉下拉鍊，馬上一泡尿直衝而出。他一邊尿一邊說：「這個電梯沒有漏水孔，所以妳們可能得站起來免得被我淹到。真的歹勢啦！可是我真的不行了！」

      A puddle was already spreading from his corner of the lift, and his stream of pee was showing no sign of stopping. Lorraine scrambled to her feet and hurriedly slipped her shoes back on to keep her feet from getting wet, any sympathy she might have had for Roger vanishing as a desperate need to pee hit her when she didn't have the pressure of her heel between her legs.
